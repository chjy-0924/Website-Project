Our Go-To Unwinding Activities:

By: Cherry Joy Toribio

As an artistic person, my go-to activities when I want to relax are either painting or writing calligraphy. I find these not only as a way to practice and enhance my skills but also to express, through art, what I am currently feeling or the situations I am experiencing. I can write calligraphy both in the traditional way and digitally, which is why some of my colleagues ask why I didn’t pursue Multimedia Arts. Aside from art, there are times when I go to a nearby coffee shop with my friends, and we always talk about our academics. In this way, I can express or rant about my hardships at school, which helps ease my burden to some extent.

By: Raven Nolasco

One of my Go-To unwinding activities is currently one of my hobbies which is playing different types of games. Lately, I’ve been interested in buying Baldur’s Gate 3, a turn-based RPG based on the system of Dungeons & Dragons. I have watched streams and videos on Youtube about the game and it piqued my interest. I’m also currently finishing an action-adventure game called Alice: Madness Returns. It’s an Alice in Wonderland inspired game with a dark and horror twist of a story which I’m into. Aside from that, I’m also into watching movies or anime series to relax or unwind at my home. I am currently watching Dan Da Dan, it’s a supernatural anime series with a little bit of comedic twist. Aside from movies or anime series, I am also into watching gameplays of some of my favorite Youtubers. One of my favorite Youtubers is Jay from Kubzscouts (yes, that’s the name of his channel), watching his hilarious and humorous gameplays takes the stress away.

By: Dwayne Fajardo

I often play video games in my free time. I tend to play various games like Tekken, Valorant, call of duty games and a lot more. The thing about me is playing has always been my passion, thus I tend to perform above average on numerous games. Because of that I am most likely always playing competitively for self improvement rather than just for fun. I also play Billiards when I have the chance.

By: Daniel Isaac Melo

My go to unwinding activities is playing guitar and playing video games with friends when I get bored or finishing my task in school I just play guitar and singing while playing guitar it kinda makes me calm when I feel pressured and when I feel tired and after I finished playing guitar I play games like single player games multiplayer games and etc. these stuffs it makes me calm when I feel tired and when I feel bored I get to do them and you know it makes me happy to knowing that I will improve in my guitar skills and having my brain rest while I do this activities.
