Sinag Tamaraw Opening Concert

Event Date: November 15, 2024

Highlights from the Event


The Sinag Tamaraw Concert was the highlight of the school year, a spectacular fusion of live music, vibrant school spirit, and delightful food stalls. It was an unforgettable night where students, faculty, alumni, and music enthusiasts gathered to celebrate the power of music and unity. With an impressive lineup of guest bands, including "We Just Changed," "Lampara," "Simpatiko PH," "AVI," and special guests "Kiyo" and "Sugarcane," the concert brought an eclectic mix of genres that resonated with everyone in attendance.

Of course, The Sinag Tamaraw Concert was more than just a performance; it was a celebration of community, creativity, and the vibrant spirit of the school. With the incredible performances by the guest bands, the diverse and delicious food options and goodies, and the undeniable school pride that filled the air, the concert became a testament to the power of music, unity, and shared experiences.

The event brought together students, alumni, and guests from all walks of life to celebrate talent, creativity, and the joy of coming together to support one another. From the lively performances to the amazing concessionaires, every detail was designed to create an unforgettable experience.

As the last notes echoed through the night and the crowd slowly began to disperse, it was clear that the Sinag Tamaraw Concert had achieved its purpose—not only by showcasing incredible talent but by bringing people together in a shared celebration of music, food, and school spirit. This was more than just a concert; it was an experience that would be remembered and cherished for years to come.



Sinag Sports Event

Event Date: November 20-22, 2024

Highlights from the Event

Every year, students eagerly await one of the most exciting events on the school calendar: the Sinag Sports. It’s a time when the campus transforms into a vibrant arena of teamwork, competition, and school pride. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or someone who's just looking to have fun, Sinag sports is an event that brings everyone together to celebrate the spirit of sportsmanship and camaraderie. The adrenaline, the cheers, the rivalries—everything about this event makes it an unforgettable experience!

Since the first day of the event, the energy in the air is palpable. Students from different courses come together to form teams, each representing their respective colors and tribes. For some, it's all about winning, but for many, it’s about showing up, supporting their friends, and relishing in the thrill of friendly competition.

Whether it’s basketball, volleyball, badminton, table tennis, there is always a wide variety of sports to join from. These events don’t just showcase athletic skill, but also emphasize values like teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. Every match, every game, and every cheer is a testament to the hard work and training students have put in, all leading up to this grand day.

But beyond the competition, the real magic of Sinag Sports lies in the way it fosters unity. The event breaks down barriers between students of all backgrounds, bringing them together in a shared pursuit of fun and excitement. You might find yourself teaming up with someone you’ve never spoken to before, but by the end of the day, you’re laughing, cheering, and high-fiving like old friends. The event teaches us that sports are not just about winning, but about bonding, enjoying the moment, and making memories.
