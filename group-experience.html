Our Group Experience in this Project

By: Toribio, Nolasco, Fajardo, and Melo

Visual Archive


Creating this project allowed us to explore web design and teamwork while overcoming challenges. Developing a website was difficult and overwhelming at first. However, as we progressed, we couldn't deny that we had fun experimenting, coding, debugging, and developing our website until we finished. We know we still have a lot to improve on, but we're grateful for this experience and the lessons we’ve learned, which we will surely carry throughout our college journey.

Cherry Joy Toribio (Web Development Manager)

Cherry Joy Toribio played a pivotal role in the success of our website development as the Web Development Manager. She demonstrated exceptional leadership and organizational skills, ensuring that every team member understood their roles and responsibilities. From the project's inception, she meticulously planned the workflow, set realistic timelines, and coordinated tasks among the team. Cherry Joy also maintained consistent communication with everyone involved, fostering collaboration and addressing any challenges that arose. Her strategic oversight ensured that our project stayed on track and aligned with our goals, delivering a functional and visually appealing website within the agreed timeframe.

Raven S. Nolasco (Content Manager)

Raven Nolasco brought life to our website through her creative content management. She was responsible for curating and creating all the written materials and visual elements that enriched the website's appeal. From crafting engaging articles to photographing images that perfectly complemented the content, Raven ensured that the website's tone and visuals resonated with our target audience. Her dedication to quality content and eye for detail added depth and personality to our website, making it both informative and captivating.

Dwayne Fajardo (Web Designer)

Dwayne Fajardo’s expertise in web design gave our website its professional and polished look. He was responsible for the visual layout and CSS styling, ensuring that the design was both aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly. By creating a cohesive color scheme, typography, and responsive layouts, Dwayne enhanced the overall user experience. His attention to detail ensured that every visual element aligned with our brand identity.

Daniel Isaac Melo (Technical Lead)

Daniel Isaac Melo provided the solid foundation for our website by focusing on its organization and technical framework. He handled the HTML coding and ensured that the website's structure was functional and efficient. His technical knowledge and precision were critical in creating a seamless and accessible web platform.

Special thanks to:Hannah Gabrielle Caling

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to Hannah Gabrielle Caling, our senior advisor, whose expert guidance and invaluable suggestions greatly influenced our website's success. Her mentorship and problem-solving skills were instrumental in overcoming challenges and refining our final product.
