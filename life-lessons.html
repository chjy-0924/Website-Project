Life Lessons in this College Journey

By: Toribio, Nolasco, Fajardo, and Melo

Visual Archive


A fantastic Halloween event was held at FEU Roosevelt Marikina on October 29, 2024, an event made to celebrate Halloween and to let the students unleash their creativity with their creative Cosplays. Spooky decorations and eye-catching costumes were everywhere throughout the campus. Students from Kindergarten to TED students, and other teachers and professors participated and were dressed up as their preferred costumes aligned with the season.

Lots of thrilling designs and decorations over the hallways caught the attention of the students, it is definitely made with lots of effort! The attention to details in these lobby decorations are all well thought out and captured the essence of the Halloween season. A Halloween Cosplay competition was also held for students to participate to showcase their talents in the event by dressing up as their preferred characters from different genres may it be a cartoon character or a video game character.
